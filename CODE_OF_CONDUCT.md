# Contributor Code of Conduct

We aim to provide a welcoming and supportive environment. Please read the full Contributor Covenant at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/ and follow its guidelines.

Key expectations:
- Be respectful and kind.
- Assume positive intent.
- Act professionally and be inclusive.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project lead or maintainer listed in HANDOFF.md.
